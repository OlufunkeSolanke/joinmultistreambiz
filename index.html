Frequently Asked Questions

Is Multistream a Ponzi scheme? +

No. Multistream TM Limited is a registered, Nigerian-based direct-selling company with real physical products, JINJA Herbal Extract and IRU Antiseptic Soap, that customers actually receive and use. Your earnings come from product sales and team building, not just from recruiting people to pay in.

How fast can I withdraw my earnings? +

Payouts are weekly. Every Friday is payday in the Multistream business model, so you do not have to wait months to see your earnings.

Can I really start with little money? +

Yes. The Entry package starts at just ₦8,700. You can start small and upgrade your package whenever you are ready.

Do I have to sell to earn? +

Not necessarily. You can join purely as a product user, just register with a package and get paid on your next repurchase of a new package. But if you want to earn from the business side, there are three paths: product reseller, distributor, or team builder. You choose what works for you.

Is JINJA Herbal Extract NAFDAC approved? +

Yes. JINJA Herbal Extract carries NAFDAC registration number A7-1733L.

Can my whole family join together? +

Yes. We have a Family/Multiple Account Package designed for households who want to build together or earn on multiple accounts. You can have as many accounts as you desire. A Single/Joint effort makes the whole family earn.

How does the Multistreams business actually work? +

It is simple. Just three steps:

Step 1 — Register. Choose a package that works for your budget, from the Entry package at ₦8,700 all the way to the Flourish package. Pay once and you are in.

Step 2 — Use or Sell. Use the products yourself and share your experience, or sell to customers around you. Either way the products do the talking.

Step 3 — Earn Weekly. Every Friday is payday. Earn from direct sales, direct referrals, team matching bonuses, rank advancements and 10 other income streams. The higher your package, the deeper your earning reach.

What next after I register? +

We do not register and abandon. From the moment you join Prime Movers Squad, you step into a community of people genuinely rooting for your success.

We run daily training and strategy sessions to keep you sharp, informed and motivated. There is always someone ready to answer your questions, walk you through the process and lock hands with you every step of the way.

The only thing we ask of you is to come willing, hungry and coachable. If you bring that, we bring everything else. Your success is our success.
